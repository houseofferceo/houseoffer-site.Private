Buying hub

The smart way to buy a home
in England & Wales

Buying a home is the biggest purchase most people ever make — and almost no one is taught how to do it well. Estate agents are experts who do this every day; the buyer on the other side of the table usually does it a handful of times in a lifetime. This hub exists to close that gap.

Every purchase comes down to three questions, in order.

1
What is this property actually worth?

Before anything else, separate the marketing price from the real one.

See what a property is really worth →
2
What should you offer to win?

Value tells you the ground; your offer is the strategy on top of it — balancing the risk of overpaying against the risk of losing the home.

Work out what to offer →
3
How do you keep the price as low as possible?

Once your number is set, the job is defending it — reading the seller and holding your nerve.

Learn how to negotiate the price down →
