1. What HouseOffer is

HouseOffer is a research tool that helps UK home buyers assess whether a property's asking price aligns with comparable sales and market data. You paste a property URL, we generate a report.

We use data from HM Land Registry, the ONS House Price Index, the EPC Register, and other public and commercial property data sources. We combine this with our own methodology to produce a verdict and supporting evidence.
